# **App Name**: AutoGuard EHS

## Core Features:

- Employee Profile Management: Create, manage, and store employee profiles, including contact information, job roles, and safety certifications.
- Activity/Training Scheduling: Schedule and assign safety activities and training sessions to employees based on their roles and responsibilities.
- Training Completion Tracking: Monitor and track employee progress and completion of assigned training modules. LLM Tool determines necessary remedial actions if training completion is delayed or incomplete.
- Detailed Safety Records: Maintain and provide access to detailed employee safety records, including completed training, incident reports, and safety performance.
- Reporting and Analytics Dashboard: Generate EHS compliance and safety training status reports. Dashboards for visualizing key performance indicators (KPIs).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#3F704D) to evoke safety, health, and environmental consciousness.
- Background color: Light gray (#E9E9E9) for a clean, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted olive (#808000) to add depth and visual interest without distracting from the main color scheme.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality
- Use simple, clear icons to represent safety procedures, training modules, and employee roles.
- Clean and organized layout with clear visual hierarchy to ensure ease of use and quick access to information.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience without being distracting.
